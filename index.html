Features
Everything you need to find the deal.
Built for investors who do their own research and move fast.
🏆

DealScore™

Every property gets a score from 0–100 based on undervaluation, cap rate, cash-on-cash return, market momentum, and value-add upside.

🗺️

Live Deal Map

See every deal plotted on a real interactive map. Price pins colored by grade — green for A deals, blue for B, orange for C.

📊

Full Investment Analysis

Cap rate, cash-on-cash return, net monthly cash flow, down payment required, closing costs — all calculated automatically.

🔍

Smart Filters

Filter by price range, beds/baths, cap rate, strategy (BRRRR, flip, cash flow), DealScore, and more. Find your deal in seconds.

👤

Owner Data

See owner name, type, estimated equity, and tax records on every property. Know who to contact before anyone else does.

📥

Export & Save

Export any search to CSV in one click. Save searches and get notified when new deals match your criteria.

How it works
From search to offer in minutes.
1

Search any market

Type a city, ZIP code, or county. DealScope pulls all active listings and runs them through the algorithm instantly.

2

DealScore ranks the deals

Every property gets scored 0–100. Sort by DealScore and the best deals float to the top automatically.

3

Analyze, save, and act

Open any property for a full investment breakdown, owner data, and AI insights. Export to CSV or save to a list.
